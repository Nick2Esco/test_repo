LET US INTRODUCE OURSELVES

It all started with Tequila...

Nick Escobar, Ryan Hankins, and Matthew Moore would get together to try a different Mexican restaurant every week.
They would get an appetizer, dinner, and three margaritas to subside their thirst. Soon enough, the three would come to realize what love really is...
a healthy mixture of good food, strong friends, and even stronger tequila.

Love created Zesco's Tequileria, and after five years and numerous awards the tequileria has become a prominent hot spot for the city of Charleston, South Carolina.
Food has been served, tequila shots have been thrown down, and memories have been made.
In the end, the three friends could not be anymore prouder of what they have created.
Especially now, since they get to drink for free.
